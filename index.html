Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website project typically take? +

Most website projects take 4-8 weeks depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom applications may take 8-12 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline after understanding your requirements.

What technologies do you use? +

We use modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, PHP, WordPress, MongoDB, MySQL, AWS, and more. We choose the best technology stack based on your project requirements and long-term goals.

Do you provide support after project completion? +

Yes! We offer comprehensive support packages including maintenance, updates, bug fixes, security patches, and optimization. Our team is available 24/7 for any urgent issues.

What is your pricing structure? +

Our pricing is customized based on your project scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer flexible payment plans including fixed-price projects, hourly rates, and retainer packages. Request a free quote for detailed pricing.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing? +

Absolutely! We provide comprehensive SEO services, content strategy, social media marketing, email campaigns, and paid advertising. Our goal is to drive qualified traffic and generate real business results.

Do you work with small businesses and startups? +

Yes! We love working with startups and small businesses. We understand budget constraints and offer scalable solutions that grow with your business. Many of our best client relationships started with small projects.
