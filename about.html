Who We Are

CodeinBrain is a full-service digital solutions company founded by Hariom Kumbhkar in 2016 with a mission to help businesses of all sizes establish a powerful digital presence and drive meaningful growth.

We are a team of passionate developers, innovative designers, and strategic digital marketers who believe in the power of technology to transform businesses. Over the past 2 years, we've helped 18+ clients across various industries achieve their digital goals through CodeinBrain's innovative solutions and strategies.

Our expertise spans website development, mobile applications, UI/UX design, e-commerce solutions, digital marketing, and comprehensive IT support. We don't just build websites and apps – we build partnerships with our clients. Founded by Hariom Kumbhkar, CodeinBrain continues to deliver exceptional results with a focus on AI-driven and scalable digital products.
